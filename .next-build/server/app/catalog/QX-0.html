Getting Started

Assembly & ordering

1

Unpack frame

Remove frame components from packaging. Verify all parts against the included checklist.

2

Assemble legs

Click-lock the column tubes into the crossbar. No tools required for this step.

3

Attach feet

Screw levelling feet into column bases. Adjust for uneven floors as needed.

4

Mount desktop

Place desktop face-down on soft surface. Align frame and secure with 6 included screws.

5

Connect power

Route motor cables through cable spine. Connect control box and plug into mains power.

6

Flip & calibrate

Carefully flip desk upright. Run auto-calibration by holding ▼ button for 5 seconds.
